Monte Carlo
Simulates thousands of possible price paths using four mathematical models
Probability a stock hits a target, what an option is worth, how much you could lose in the worst 5% of scenarios
You want to price an option, estimate portfolio risk, or stress-test a trade before entering
Live
IV Surface
Builds a 3D map of implied volatility across all strikes and expiry dates from live options data
Whether options are cheap or expensive, where big moves are expected, and what traders fear most
You're trading options, hedging, or want to understand what the market is pricing in right now
Live
Portfolio
Finds the mathematically optimal mix of assets for the best return at a given risk level
How much of each asset to hold, your expected return vs risk, and whether your current allocation is efficient
You're building or rebalancing and want data-driven allocation, not guesswork
Live
Fixed Income
Models the live US Treasury yield curve and prices bonds and interest rate swaps against it
What a bond is worth at any rate, how sensitive it is to rate changes, and whether a swap is in or out of the money
You hold bonds, trade rates, or need to understand what a Fed move does to your fixed income book
Live
Pairs Trading
Scans for stock pairs that historically move together and signals when they've diverged enough to trade
Which pairs are stretched apart (enter), which have converged (exit), and historical profitability of each pair
You want market-neutral trade ideas that profit from mean-reversion regardless of market direction
Live
Vol & Regime
Detects whether markets are in a low-vol, high-vol, or crisis regime using statistical models on live data
Current volatility regime, 60-day forecast with confidence bands, how returns look historically in each regime
You want to size positions correctly, time hedges, or understand the statistical market environment
Live
Alpha & Execution
Breaks down a stock's returns into market/size/value factors and optimises how to execute large trades
How much return is genuine alpha vs market exposure, and the cheapest way to trade a large block without moving the price
You're evaluating strategy alpha, building a factor model, or trading a large position needing careful execution
Live
Credit Risk
Estimates probability a company defaults using its live equity price and balance sheet data
How far a company is from default, what its credit spread should be, and CVA on a derivatives position
You're buying corporate bonds, trading CDS, or assessing counterparty risk on a derivatives portfolio
Live
